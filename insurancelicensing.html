INSURANCE & LICENSING

The right insurance solutions for your peace of mind

Nagarkot facilitates cargo insurance solutions at competitive rates.

It is highly recommended that you protect your cargo with insurance to avoid any possible risk of total or partial damage of your cargo while in transit.

We handle cargo insurance for one-time transportation and even offer long-term insurance contracts covering specified cargo being transported during the contract term. Cargo can be insured against almost all damages, destruction or other losses.
