Why Nagarkot?

With continually shifting international trade landscape and customs regulations, delays can be both frustrating and expensive, not to mention detrimental to your business reputation.

An experienced, reliable and compliance-driven customs broker keeps you ahead of regulatory and documentation challenges, ensuring that your cargo makes a smooth transit.

We understand each element of the supply chain and tailor processes to your needs to ensure hassle-free movement of goods, free from delays or penalties.

Our Services Include

  • Export and Import Declaration Filing: Customs declarations to process exports / imports
  • Security Filing: Advance notifications to authorities comprising of information required
    for security screening
  • Duties & Taxes: Payment facilitation on behalf of client
  • In-Transit Movement: Transportation of uncleared cargo within Customs territory
  • Duty Drawback, Fiscal representation, Excise Duty Handling, and others
  • Other: Competitive access to FTZ and bonded warehouse facilities
